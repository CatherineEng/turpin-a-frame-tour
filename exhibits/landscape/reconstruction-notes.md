# Turpin Digital Exhibit — Phase 1

## Overview

This directory contains **Phase 1 of the Turpin Digital Exhibit**, a reconstructed and narrated presentation of an archaeological interpretation originally experienced as a live, social XR environment built using Mozilla Hubs.

Phase 1 is designed as a **durable, inspectable, and citable scholarly object**, independent of any live platform. It presents the reconstructed spatial environments alongside interpretive guidance derived from surviving assets, archival recordings, and authorial intent.

This phase prioritizes **long-term accessibility, clarity of interpretation, and provenance**, while preserving the ability to support a future live, multi-user experience (Phase 2).

---

## Relationship to the Original Live Environment

From approximately **[YEAR–YEAR]**, the Turpin reconstruction existed as a live Mozilla Hubs environment that included:

- real-time social navigation  
- spatialized voice  
- in-room interpretive signage  
- pinned audio and media elements  
- guided tours and classroom use  

That live environment no longer exists in its original hosted form.

The materials in this directory are **derived from the surviving export of that environment**, which includes:

- Spoke-authored scene geometry  
- room-level configuration data  
- uploaded media assets  
- archival video documentation of live walkthroughs  

Not all elements of the live experience were embedded in the scene geometry itself. Where necessary, interpretive components have been **reconstructed transparently** based on available evidence.

---

## What Phase 1 Is

Phase 1 is:

- a **single-user, web-based exhibit**  
- focused on **spatial understanding and interpretation**  
- intended for **scholarly reference, teaching, and public access**  
- stable and platform-independent  
- explicit about sources, assumptions, and reconstruction decisions  

Each exhibit includes:
- a canonical 3D scene (`.glb`)  
- optional narration and interpretive guidance  
- documentation of provenance and reconstruction choices  

---

## What Phase 1 Is Not

Phase 1 is **not**:

- a recreation of the original live, social XR experience  
- a replacement for in-person or live guided tours  
- dependent on Mozilla Hubs, Reticulum, or real-time infrastructure  

Live, multi-user interaction — including spatial voice and shared presence — is considered a **separate, additive layer** and is intentionally deferred to a future phase.
